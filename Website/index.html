DSAT Skills Audit
As part of our first ever DSAT Business Integration Event, we decided that we wanted to get a high level view of the huge diversity of skills and interests that our team brings together. We hope that by getting a better understanding of the unqiue contributions that each team makes that we will be a stronger and more cohesive whole. We've set up a (hopefully!) smooth analytical pipeline which means that your results will be anonymously visualised on screen with the others as soon as you submit them.

We've asked for your work email address so that we can make sure that no duplicates are submitted and aggregate the answers at a team level. We will also be sharing the non-personal data with our team leaders so that they can work out how we can bring our skills together in the best way.

Thank you for taking part in the survey!
